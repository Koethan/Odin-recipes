Arroz con leche

A delicious Cuban dessert, Arroz Con Leche.

This rich and creamy rice pudding is packed with the flavors of cinnamon, anise, and lime zest, which give this dessert a stunning uplift. You will definitely love this easy and perfect dessert to end your favorite Cuban meal.

Arroz Con Leche Ingredients

  • Valencia white rice (Can NOT be long grain rice!)
  • Whole milk
  • Sea salt
  • Cinnamon stick
  • Lime peel or lemon peel
  • Star anise seeds
  • Condensed milk
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon for garnish

Arroz Con Leche Steps

  1. In a saucepan, over medium heat, mix the rinsed rice, 5 cups of whole milk, cinnamon stick, lemon peel, anise seeds and salt.
  2. Once the milk is mostly evaporated, add an additional cup of milk and stir to incorporate.
  3. Stir in butter and sugar into the cooked rice.
  4. Add in the condensed milk and cook for another 10 minutes.
  5. Spoon into individual serving dishes or eat it straight out of the pan, no one will judge you. Sprinkle with cinnamon and chill for about an hour before serving.
